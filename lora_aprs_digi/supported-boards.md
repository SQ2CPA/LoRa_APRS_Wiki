The **LoRa APRS Digi/iGate** firmware supports a variety of commercially available LoRa development boards as well as DIY configurations. Below is the list of officially supported hardware.

> We don't support a vast number of devices, but the ones we do are well-tested and proven to be stable. Our focus is on quality over quantity. If a new, popular, and stable device emerges and is widely adopted by the community, it will likely be added and fully supported in the future.
