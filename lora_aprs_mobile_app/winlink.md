The app includes built-in **Winlink** support, letting you send and receive Winlink emails directly over RF.

> **⚠️ Experimental feature!** Winlink support is **experimental**. It may not always work as expected and its behavior can change in future updates. Use it with that in mind.

---

## Accessing Winlink

Open the **Messages** section. In the bottom-left corner of the messages screen you will find the **Winlink button** (blue envelope icon).

<img src="/assets/images/lora_aprs_mobile_app/winlink/access-winlink.jpg" alt="Winlink button in the bottom-left of the Messages screen" style="max-width: 300px; width: 100%;">

Tapping this button opens the Winlink screen.

<img src="/assets/images/lora_aprs_mobile_app/winlink/winlink.jpg" alt="Winlink screen" style="max-width: 300px; width: 100%;">

---

## Settings (Winlink password)

Before you can use Winlink, you must log in with your **Winlink password**. Tap the **settings icon (gear)** in the top-right corner of the Winlink screen. A modal will appear where you enter your Winlink password and press **Save**.

<img src="/assets/images/lora_aprs_mobile_app/winlink/winlink-settings.jpg" alt="Winlink password modal" style="max-width: 300px; width: 100%;">

Until a password is set, the screen shows a **"Not logged in"** warning and the feature stays locked.

---

## Sending Winlink commands and messages

At the bottom of the Winlink screen, above the message input, there is a row of quick command buttons such as **L**, **R#**, **Y#**, **SP**, **SMS**, **F#**, **K#** and more. These are standard Winlink commands you can send with a single tap:

- **L** – list messages
- **R#** – read message number `#`
- and other Winlink commands.

Besides these commands, you can also **type and send messages normally** using the message input field at the bottom.
