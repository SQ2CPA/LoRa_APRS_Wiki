The **Friends** tab lets you keep a list of stations you care about. You can quickly see when each of your friends was last heard, how far away they are, and jump straight into a conversation with them.

---

## Accessing the Friends Tab

On the **Main Screen**, in the **Messages Section**, you will find the **Friends icon** (two people) next to the messages and new message buttons.

<img src="/assets/images/lora_aprs_mobile_app/friends/access.jpg" alt="Friends icon in the Messages Section" style="max-width: 300px; width: 100%;">

The icon shows a badge with the number of currently **active friends** (friends that have been heard recently).

---

## Friends Screen

Tapping the Friends icon opens the Friends screen.

<img src="/assets/images/lora_aprs_mobile_app/friends/friends.jpg" alt="Friends screen" style="max-width: 300px; width: 100%;">

- **Add a friend (top)**: Enter the callsign of your friend, with a specific SSID if needed (e.g. `SP1ABC-9`), and press **Add**. Add mobile stations only — trackers and handhelds. Avoid adding igates and digipeaters.
- **Recent contacts**: A few of the last stations you were in contact with are shown here. Just tap a callsign to add it to your friends list instantly.
- **Friends list**: Below that you will see the list of your friends.

---

## Friends List

<img src="/assets/images/lora_aprs_mobile_app/friends/friends-info.jpg" alt="Friend list entry details" style="max-width: 300px; width: 100%;">

Each entry in the list shows:

- **Callsign** of the friend.
- **Last activity** - how long ago the station was last heard.
- **Distance** - how far away the station currently is.

For each friend you also have:

- **Message button (green chat icon)**: Opens a conversation with that friend.
- **Info button (info icon)**: Opens detailed information about the station.
- **Delete button (trash icon)**: Removes the friend from your list.
