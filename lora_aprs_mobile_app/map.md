The Map lets you visualize stations directly on an interactive map inside the app.

---

## Accessing the Map

On the **Home** (Main Screen), there is a **map button** in the **top-right corner** of the screen. Tapping it takes you straight to the Map.

<img src="/assets/images/lora_aprs_mobile_app/map/access-map.jpg" alt="Map button in the top-right corner of the Main Screen header" style="max-width: 300px; width: 100%;">

---

## Symbols

**All APRS symbols are fully supported** and displayed on the map, so every station shows up with its correct icon.

<img src="/assets/images/lora_aprs_mobile_app/map/map1.jpg" alt="Map showing stations with APRS symbols" style="max-width: 300px; width: 100%;">

---

## Symbol Clustering

When many stations are located close together, the map **groups (clusters) symbols** so the view stays readable. As you zoom in, the clusters split apart into individual stations.

<img src="/assets/images/lora_aprs_mobile_app/map/map2.jpg" alt="Clustered symbols grouped together on the map" style="max-width: 300px; width: 100%;">

<img src="/assets/images/lora_aprs_mobile_app/map/map3.jpg" alt="Map view with stations spread out" style="max-width: 300px; width: 100%;">

---

## Current Limitations

At the moment, the Map does **not** yet support:

-   **Station paths / tracks** – movement history is not drawn on the map yet.
-   **Offline maps** – map tiles currently require an internet connection.

---

## Planned: Full Offline Support

The goal is for the Map to work **fully offline**, with no internet required. This matches the core idea of the whole app: to operate offline, without an internet connection.
